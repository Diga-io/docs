## Phone Numbers

Phone numbers allow your voice agents to receive and make calls. In this section, we explain how to purchase numbers directly from Diga, so you don't have to manage telephony infrastructure.

<Note>
We currently only offer Spanish phone numbers, and calls are only allowed to national numbers. If you want to make requests for other countries, check the [Configure custom number](/en/telephony/sip-trunks) section.
</Note>

## Buy Phone Numbers


<Steps>
  <Step title="Select your numbers">
    Go to **Phone Numbers** and click "Buy number". Select the province and the number of numbers you need.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/comprar-numero.png" alt="Screenshot of Diga's phone number purchase interface" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>


  <Step title="Complete payment">
    You will be redirected to Stripe to process the payment securely. The numbers will be reserved for your project.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your identity (first purchase)">
    If this is your first purchase, you must complete the identity verification process before the numbers are activated. You have 24 hours to complete this step.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your agents">
    Once active, assign agents to your numbers so they can receive and make calls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Numbers reserved during purchase will be kept on hold until identity verification is completed.
</Note>

## Identity Verification

### Why is it Necessary?

Identity verification is a **regulatory requirement** to prevent fraud and comply with telecommunications regulations. It is only necessary for the first number purchase by each company.

### When is it Required

Verification is mandatory in these cases:

- **First number purchase**: New companies purchasing their first number
- **No previous verification**: If your company never completed verification

<Info>
Companies already verified can purchase additional numbers without additional verification.
</Info>

### Verification Process

Identity is verified with a **digitally signed document**. You download a verification document, sign it with your company or self-employed (autónomo) digital certificate, and upload it to confirm ownership of the number. The process is as follows:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete your purchase">
    After entering your payment information, your numbers will be temporarily reserved. You won't be charged until your identity is verified.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download the document">
    A verification step will appear with a document to download.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign with your digital certificate">
    Sign the document using your company or self-employed (autónomo) digital certificate.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload the signed document">
    Upload the signed document to validate ownership of the number.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for approval">
    Your number will be reviewed in less than 24 hours. You will receive all the information by email.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Automatic activation">
    Once approved, your reserved numbers will be automatically activated.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Verification States

Your verification can be in one of these states:

**Pending**
- Documents submitted, awaiting review
- You cannot purchase additional numbers during this period
- Purchased numbers remain reserved

**Verified**
- Successful approval
- Reserved numbers automatically activated
- You can purchase additional numbers without restrictions

**Rejected**
- Signed document not approved
- Payment automatically canceled (you are not charged)
- Reserved numbers released
- You can try to verify again with a correctly signed document

## Number Lifecycle

Phone numbers go through different states during their lifetime in your project.

### Number States

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Reserved">
    The number is temporarily assigned to your project during the purchase or verification process.

    - Not yet active for calls
    - Remains in this state during verification
    - Automatically activated upon verification approval
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Assigned (Active)">
    The number is fully active and operational.

    - You can assign agents for inbound and outbound calls
    - Billed monthly (€1.95/month + VAT)
    - Appears in your list of active numbers
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Released (Quarantine)">
    You have released the number, but it is in a 30-day quarantine period.

    - You cannot use it for calls
    - Other customers cannot purchase it during this period
    - Still visible in your panel (marked as "In quarantine")
    - Agents are automatically unassigned
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Available">
    After quarantine, the number becomes available for purchase again.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Quarantine Period

When you release a number, it enters a **30-day quarantine period** before being available to other customers.

**Why does quarantine exist?**
- Prevents recently released numbers from being immediately purchased by other companies
- Allows time for contacts to update their records
- Reduces confusion for customers who may have the number saved

<Warning>
During quarantine, the number can no longer be used for calls and is not billed. You cannot reactivate a released number. If you need to recover it, [contact support](https://www.diga.io/contact).
</Warning>


## Number Billing

### Monthly Cost

Each Diga phone number has a fixed cost:

- **€1.95 + VAT per number per month**
- Monthly billing independent of your subscription plan
- Charged along with your monthly minutes invoice

## Troubleshooting

### I cannot purchase additional numbers

**Cause**: Your company has a pending verification.

**Solution**:
- Complete the identity verification process
- Once approved, you can purchase additional numbers

### My payment was authorized but the numbers are not active

**Cause**: You have a pending identity verification.

**Explanation**:
- Payment is authorized but not captured
- Numbers are reserved awaiting verification
- They will be automatically activated upon verification approval

### My verification was rejected

**Solution**:
1. Review the reason for rejection in the email you received
2. Re-sign the verification document with a valid company or self-employed (autónomo) digital certificate
3. Retry the verification process
4. The previous payment was canceled, you were not charged

### I released a number by mistake

<Warning>
Released numbers cannot be recovered. They enter a 30-day quarantine and then become available to other customers.
</Warning>

If you released a number by mistake:
- It is not possible to reactivate it
- You can purchase a different number immediately
- The released number will not be available to others until after 30 days
