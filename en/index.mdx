## What is Diga?

Diga is a no-code platform designed to be **simple, powerful, and easy to adopt.** Whether you're part of a consulting firm, agency, SME, or any type of business that needs to automate calls, here you'll find the information you need to create and manage voice agents that work naturally and efficiently.

In this documentation you'll learn how to configure agents, manage projects with independent workspaces and billing, integrate external systems via MCP or custom APIs, and connect phone numbers — either purchased on the platform or your own — through SIP trunking.

Our goal is to provide you with a clear guide so you can work with Diga autonomously and confidently.
We're glad to have you here. Explore, create, and grow your business.
